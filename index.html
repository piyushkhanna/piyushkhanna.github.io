HASHBBC


Achint Maniar, also known as HASHBBC, is a rising electronic music producer, songwriter, and DJ. Catapulting onto the scene with full force, Chicago-based artist HASHBBC is no stranger to attacking all spectrums of bass music especially known for his enigmatic future bass and Dubstep sets.

About Me

With a tasteful discography of original productions, HASHBBC has built a loyal following and received strong support from high-profile artists throughout the industry. HASHBBC’s original productions have landed him massive support slots for ARMNHMR, Synymata, The Midnight, and many more. HASHBBC’s production has also earned him label placements on TheSoul Publishing, Sizeland, PYRO, and Semantic Sounds. HASHBBC shows no signs of slowing down as he has landed massive support from artists like MusicByLukas, Lost Stories, Liquid Todd, Hollywood Hamilton, DJ Fenix, Mojojojo, and so on. He has also been featured for his songs on major music news websites such as Rolling Stone India, Dancing Astronaut, Rave Jungle, and EDM.com and his music has been played on major radio channels like B.B.C. Asia, Dash Radio, iHeartRadio, and Kiss FM. Lets keep this going!
